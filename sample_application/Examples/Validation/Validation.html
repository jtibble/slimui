Validation

A simple form with static validation can be created like so:

Username:
Password:
Username should be 8-20 letters or numbers
Password should be 8-20 letters or numbers

Complex Validation

A domain-object form can be created and validated using a schema, like this customer-form below:

Customer Name
Customer Address
Customer City/State/Zip
Customer SSN
Customer Date of Birth
Years Driving
Customer Name is required
Customer Address is required
Customer City is required
Customer State is required
Customer Zip is required
Customer Zip must be formatted correctly (##### or #####-####)
Customer SSN must be formatted correctly (###-##-####)
Customer must be 18 years old
Customer years-driven must be an integer
Customer years-driven must be a number
Customer years-driven cannot be negative
